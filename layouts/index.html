Bio

Daoping Wang is an Associate Professor at the School of Finance, Nankai University. His research focuses on Financial Big Data Analysis, AI and Large Language Models in Finance, International Finance, Financial Risk and Regulation, and Sustainable Finance.

He has published papers in leading international journals including Pacific-Basin Finance Journal, Economics Letters, et al.
